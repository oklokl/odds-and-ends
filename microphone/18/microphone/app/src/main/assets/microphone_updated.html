Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

The application collects and uses the following data and permissions for its core functionality:

  • Audio Recordings / Voice Data: The application requires microphone access to record audio, which is the app's primary function. All audio data is stored locally on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.
  • Phone State: The application requests access to your phone state to enhance your user experience. This allows the app to automatically pause a recording when you receive an incoming call. The app does not access call details or record any part of your conversation.
  • Storage Access: The application needs permission to read and write to your device's storage to save your recordings and access them for playback.
  • Bluetooth: The application requests Bluetooth permission to allow recording from connected Bluetooth devices, such as wireless microphones or headsets.
  • Notifications: The application may send notifications to show the current recording status and provide quick controls, which requires notification permissions.
  • File Metadata / Storage Paths: Metadata such as storage paths, creation dates, and timestamps may be processed locally on the device to support app operation.
  • App Settings and Preferences: The application may store configuration information such as sampling rate, file format, and noise reduction options to ensure proper function.

The Company will retain Your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. All user-generated data, such as audio recordings, is stored locally on your device and can be managed or deleted by you at any time.
